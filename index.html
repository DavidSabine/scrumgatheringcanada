About Us

Ontario Scrum Community® and Scrum Alliance® have partnered to support the growing community of Scrum & Agile practitioners in Canada.

Volunteers, sponsors, and community leaders have combined their efforts to launch the 1st-ever Regional Scrum Gathering® to be held in Canada. We look forward to seeing you in March, 2018.

What is Our Goal?

Organized locally. Supported globally.

Tens-of-thousands of Canadians have been working diligently to Transform the World of Work. Regional Scrum Gathering® Canada connects Scrum Alliance® members to the larger community and celebrates the contributions of all Agile & Scrum practitioners.

  • Learn new techniques and skills
  • Enjoy discussion with experts
  • Mingle with colleagues
  • Expand your network

Event Partner

Scrum Alliance® is a professional membership & certification organization in the Agile community having a mission to "Transform the World of Work" by guiding and inspiring individuals, leaders, and organizations with practices, principles, and values that create joyful, prosperous, and sustainable workplaces.

Canadian Scrum practitioners have contributed to that shared vision for many years. Ontario Scrum Community® has established this partnership with Scrum Alliance® to celebrate the incredible knowledge, diversity, and passion of our Canadian colleagues.

Event FAQs

What is the price of the ticket ?

Approximately ~$350

You can help! The more sponsors we get, the lower the price. Please spread the word within your organization and tell us who to contact about sponsorship.

We are working diligently to ensure the event is affordable no matter how far you'll travel.

We aim to open registrations in Summer 2017 or earlier.

What is included in my registration ?

Fun & Food

We are working out the details.

Our plan is to ensure access to great food and excellent content throughout the event. Hotel accommodations and travel will not be included in the registration fee but we are negotiating with hotels to get you the best possible price.

Also note, if you can help us raise funds through sponsorships, we can keep the ticket prices as low as possible.

Why Toronto ? Did you consider _____ ?

It's All About the Data

Roughly 48% of Canadian Scrum Alliance® members live in Toronto. (Not including the Greater Toronto Area.)

The organizing committee includes members from across the country and we carefully considered the location of this event.

We can achieve 2 important objectives in this 1st year by hosting the event in Toronto:

  • We can develop relationships with Canada's largest corporations who have national presence to secure sponsorships for this and (hopefully) future events.
  • We can explore ideas to strengthen the networks and raise awareness of Scrum in other Canadian communities.

Why Spring, Not Earlier ?

We Want to Complement, Not Compete

With Toronto chosen as the location, we wanted to be careful not to compete with other events in the calendar. We tip our hat to the incredible success of:

  • Toronto Agile Community
  • Agile Coach Camp (East & West)
  • Play4Agile
  • Gatineau Ottawa Agile Tour

All of those great events occur between June & November and are organized by our hard-working friends & colleauges. Regional Scrum Gathering® Canada is scheduled to not conflict with those events. We intend to augment the calendar with additional learning opportunities for our growing community.
