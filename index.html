Title Sponsor

Scrum Alliance® is a professional membership & certification organization in the Agile community having a mission to "Transform the World of Work" by guiding and inspiring individuals, leaders, and organizations with practices, principles, and values that create joyful, prosperous, and sustainable workplaces.

Scrum Alliance® hosts global gatherings worldwide several times per year and supports regional gatherings hosted by regional/local user groups.

Canadian Scrum practitioners have contributed to that shared vision for many years. Ontario Scrum Community® appreciates the support of Scrum Alliance® as we celebrate the incredible knowledge, diversity, and passion of our Canadian colleagues.
